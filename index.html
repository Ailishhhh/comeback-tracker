📜 The 4 Rules (read before anything)

1. Never a zero-day. Beast-mode when you can, but on dead days do the Bad-Day Floor (25 min). Missing one day is life; missing two builds the wrong habit.
2. Same-day digestion. Whatever PW/school teaches today, revise it + do its DPP the SAME night. This single rule stops new backlog from ever forming.
3. Backlog runs in parallel. Don't wait to "finish backlog first." Every day = a bit of current + a bit of backlog. Both tracks, always.
4. Problems > reading. You learn PCM by solving, not by watching. Aim: 60% of study time should be your pen moving.

⚙️ The Daily Engine your main routine — block-based, not clock-based

Built for a school + PW-offline student. Do the blocks in any order that fits your day. On a packed day (school + PW class), Blocks 1 & 4 are the must-dos. On a free day, go big on Block 2.
